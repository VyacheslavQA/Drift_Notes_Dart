Privacy Policy of the Drift Notes App

Drift Notes (hereinafter referred to as the “App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, where it is stored, and what rights you have as a user.

1. What data we collect
We collect the following data that you voluntarily provide when using the App:
• Name, email address, phone number (for registration and login)
• Data obtained via login with Google or phone number
• Geolocation (for marking fishing spots on the map)
• Photos and text notes related to fishing
• Types of gear, weather conditions, bottom maps, and other fishing-related data
• Account settings (language, measurement units, etc.)
• IP address and device information (model, OS) used solely for diagnostics and abuse prevention

2. How we use this data
Collected data is used exclusively for:
• Operating and personalizing the App
• Displaying information on the map and in the calendar
• Bite activity analysis using OpenAI and Anthropic Claude models (analysis is done without access to personal data)
• Storing your notes and data in your account
• Improving service quality, fixing bugs, ensuring security, and preventing fraud

3. Where the data is stored
All user data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud). This data is accessible only to you and the App for downloading and displaying information.
Data is not shared with third parties. We do not sell or transfer your data to external organizations, except as required by law (e.g., court order).

4. Data deletion
You can delete any of your notes or records within the App at any time. You may also delete your account through the Drift Notes interface. Once deleted, all your data will be permanently erased and cannot be restored.

5. Children’s access
The App may be used by users of any age, including individuals under 18. We do not intentionally collect age-related information but are committed to ensuring the privacy and safety of all users.

6. Data security
Data transmission and storage are performed using modern security measures, including HTTPS, authentication, authorization, and encryption. We take reasonable steps to protect your data from leaks, loss, or unauthorized access.

7. Third-party services used
The App uses the following third-party services:
• Firebase (Google) — for data storage and authentication
• Google Maps API — for map display
• WeatherAPI — for weather data retrieval
• OpenAI API and Anthropic Claude — for bite activity analysis (without access to personal data)
• Google Play Services, App Store Services, RuStore Services — for payment processing, analytics, and update delivery

8. User rights
You have the right to:
• Access the data stored about you
• Delete specific data or your entire account
• Stop using the App at any time
• Withdraw consent for data processing (which will result in account deletion)

9. Policy updates
We may periodically update this Policy. In the case of significant changes, we will notify you through the App interface or via email.
